Our story

Since 2020

"There is no sincerer love than the love of food"

- George Bernard Shaw

As a family we have always had a love for food which came from my mother. My Mother was born in Kenya where camping, picnics and backyard open cooking was always a staple. Here at Meat & Grill all our recipes have been passed down many generations with love.

Our catering company began from a passion of cooking outdoors for family and friends creating exceptional culinary dishes. As the word spread about our delicious and innovative menus, we embarked on a journey to provide top-notch catering services.

Our approach

ALWAYS FRESH INGREDIENTS

We pride ourselves on using the highest quality ingredients available. We source our meats from trusted suppliers such as the World Famous Tom Hixon Of Smithfield’s London and the Royal Windsor Farm Shop which was awarded with the Q Guild award for Hygiene..

Focusing on freshness has resulted in our dishes to stand out for their superior quality and exceptional flavour. Our team of skilled chefs and food experts ensure that every dish is prepared to perfection.

Trust us to elevate your next event with our commitment to excellence in food.
